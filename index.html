We offer two main types of braces: conventional metal braces and clear aligners. Conventional metal braces are the most common and involve metal brackets and wires that help correct misalignments. When you have braces, it's best to avoid hard, sticky, or chewy foods as they can damage your braces or get stuck in them. Steer clear of popcorn, nuts, hard candies, chewing gum, and caramels. Additionally, avoid biting directly into hard fruits and vegetables; instead, cut them into smaller pieces. Other foods to watch out for include ice, corn on the cob, and tough meats, as they can also cause damage or discomfort.
